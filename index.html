Open Infrastructure for Scientific Research

Accelerate scientific experimentation through open-source tools, transparent governance, and collaborative innovation. Make research reproducible, accessible, and community-driven.

Core Principles

Built on transparency, openness, and collaboration

Transparency

All decisions and discussions are public. Open communication builds trust and accelerates progress.

Openness

Anyone can contribute code, documentation, or ideas. Collaboration drives innovation forward.

Governance

The Internals oversee strategy, development, and community engagement

Role Responsibility Involvement

Decision Model

Community input is encouraged; final decisions rest with the Internals. Participation does not create legal obligations or employment rights.
