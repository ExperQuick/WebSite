The Universal Challenge of Research

Every research breakthrough requires multiple trials—changing parameters, swapping out code, and iterating approaches until the requirements are met. We manage the frustration that comes with that process.

ExpFace provides frameworks to manage everything in your computational R&D. We offer the core tools and methodology to turn configuration chaos into clear, actionable data.

Specilities :
  • Fully domain-independent (from AI to quantitative analysis).
  • Completely customizable to fit your existing research codebase.
  • Flexible for both single-experiment focus and large-scale parallel trials.
  • Manage all your experiments in of fixed number dedicated *jupyter noteboks

Key Principles

Component Abstraction & Custom Workflows

Our framework enables researchers to design flexible workflows with component abstraction and customizable pipelines. This gives you the freedom to build, modify, and track your experiments with a reusable, modular approach—design once, use many times.

Declarative Code-to-Config for Architectural Sweeps

We transform large code blocks into declarative configuration dictionaries. This approach empowers you to seamlessly perform architectural sweeps, swapping entire blocks of code—not just tuning parameters—while maintaining organization and reproducibility.

Ensuring Uniqueness & Reproducibility with Hashing

Each pipeline is assigned a unique hash based on its configuration—capturing all code blocks and parameters. This ensures that every experiment is fully reproducible and can be recreated exactly, whenever needed, with precision.
